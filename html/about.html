Who Are We?

Hi! We are a community based organization, started in the flower streets of Corona del Mar, California in 2021. We are working towards the preservation of our beaches, and the overall goal of a cleaner environment. As we work towards our goals we aim to inspire people to do their part. Everybody is capable of making an impact on the world, they just have to get started. We believe in you, you need to believe in yourself! :)
