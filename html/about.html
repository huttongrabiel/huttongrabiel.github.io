Who Are We?

Hi! We are a community based organization. Our organization began in the flower streets of Corona del Mar in 2021. We are a team dedicated to improvement of the environment. Together we strive to bring together our community, as well as other communities around the world. We lead with one goal in mind, to take care of our planet.
