Join Us!

Every Monday | 8AM | Corona del Mar State Beach | Near Tackle Box Restaurant

Come get involved! We provide buckets, PPE, and trash grabbers. Bring yourself and a take charge attitude. This is an ongoing event every week and we encourage it to be added on to the schedule as a regular event.
